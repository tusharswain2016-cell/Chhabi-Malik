Who I Am

A Life Dedicated to Seva

"Service to the people of Niali is not just my duty — it is my dharma and my greatest honour."

Chhabi Malik has been a steadfast voice for the people of Niali constituency in Odisha. Rising from grassroots activism, she has dedicated her life to uplifting the most vulnerable communities — farmers, women, tribals, and the youth of the region.

Her tenure as MLA has been marked by a relentless focus on infrastructure, healthcare access, educational opportunities, and women's self-reliance. She brings a ground-level understanding to every policy debate and development initiative.

Known for her accessibility and integrity, Chhabi Malik holds regular Jan Sunwais and personally monitors the progress of every scheme in her constituency. Her leadership is rooted in listening first and acting swiftly.

Our Vision

Building a Stronger Niali

Six pillars guiding every decision, every initiative, every vote cast in the legislature on behalf of the people of Niali.

01🌾
Kisan Samridhi

Doubling farmer incomes through MSP support, crop insurance, and direct market linkages — eliminating the middlemen who squeeze our farmers.

02📚
Shiksha for All

Universal quality education with digital classrooms and full scholarship coverage for all BPL families in Niali.

03🏥
Swasth Niali

A primary health centre within 5 km of every village — free diagnostics, maternal care, and mobile medical units.

04💡
Digital Gram

High-speed internet and digital literacy to every panchayat, empowering citizens to access government services from home.

05♻️
Green Niali

Solar-powered streetlights, clean cooking fuel, and river conservation — a constituency that thrives without harming tomorrow.

06👩‍💼
Nari Shakti

30% reservation in local governance, skill centres for women, SHG credit, and zero-tolerance on crimes against women.

Political Career

The Journey So Far

From grassroots activism to the legislative assembly — a path built on purpose, not privilege.

20
Early
Community Leadership

Began grassroots social work in Niali, organising women's self-help groups, advocating for clean water access, and running adult literacy drives in underserved villages.

Panchayat
Local Governance

Elected to the Panchayati Raj system, championing rural road construction, water connections, and mid-day meal monitoring in government schools.

First Win
First MLA Election — Niali

Won the Niali assembly seat, becoming a powerful voice for farmers, fisherfolk, and daily-wage workers. Appointed to the Agriculture & Education committees.

Tenure
Legislative Contributions

Led debates on rural electrification, tribal welfare, and women's reservation. Pushed for 100% PMAY coverage and oversaw 220 km of rural road construction in the constituency.

Re-election
Re-elected — Stronger Mandate

Returned with an increased majority, reflecting people's trust. Initiated Digital Gram Yojana and Mahila Aarogya Abhiyan — two flagship programmes still running today.

Now
Serving Niali — Present Day

Focused on completing solar electrification, expanding telemedicine, and building Niali's first agro-processing cluster to create 5,000 local jobs by 2026.
